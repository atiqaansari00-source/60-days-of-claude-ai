# Day 3 - Role-Based Prompting

## What I Learned

Today I learned about Role-Based Prompting. By assigning a specific role to Claude before asking a question, the AI provides more focused, specialized, and relevant answers.

## Question Used

How can I improve my chances of getting a software engineering job as a fresher?

---

## Without Role Prompt

The response was generic and focused on common advice such as DSA, projects, GitHub, resume building, LinkedIn, and interview preparation.

---

## Founder Persona

The response focused on startup thinking, shipping products, learning in public, networking, initiative, and business impact.

---

## Developer Persona

The response focused on technical depth, DSA patterns, open source contributions, debugging skills, communication, and understanding technologies deeply.

---

## Key Learnings

1. Different roles provide different perspectives.
2. Role-Based Prompting produces more specialized answers.
3. It helps simulate advice from real-world professionals.
4. One question can generate multiple viewpoints depending on the assigned role.

## Conclusion

Role-Based Prompting is a powerful prompting technique. By assigning a role, the AI adapts its tone, priorities, and recommendations, making responses more useful and actionable.
